Widget types:

  Classic widgets:
    - Found in the tkinter package
    - Highly customisable and straighforward
    - May appear dated

    1. Label - display text
    2. Button - contain text and perform an action when clicked
    3. Entry - receives a single line of text entry
    4. Text - receives multiline text entry
    5. Frame - rectangular region to group related widgets or provide packing between them

  Themed widgets:
    - Found in the ttk submodule
    - Native look and feel for users of a given OS
    - Modern replacements for legacy widgets
    - Brand new (e.g. progress bar)
